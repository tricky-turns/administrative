Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [06/2025]

Applies to: Tricky Turns on the Pi Network

Developed by: Adaptive Studios

Tricky Turns is a casual game built on the Pi Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information when you interact with the game via the Pi Browser and Pi services like login, tipping, and leaderboards.

Information We Collect

  • Pi Network Identity: Username and public ID via Pi Login.
  • Gameplay Data: High scores, achievements, and tips.
  • Log Data: Browser/device details, usage timestamps, and error traces.

Third-Party Services

The app uses official Pi Network SDKs for login and rewards. For their privacy practices, refer to the Pi Network Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This game does not directly use cookies. However, SDKs may use them for session management. You can control cookie preferences in your browser.

Use of Information

  • Identify users through Pi Login
  • Display scores and tips
  • Improve gameplay and debug errors

We do not sell or rent any data.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee complete security. Pi Network’s platform helps protect your identity and data.

Children’s Privacy

This game is not intended for children under 13. If such data is accidentally collected, we will promptly delete it. Please contact us if you are aware of this issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here and become effective immediately.
