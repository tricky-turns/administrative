Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 06/2025

Applies to: Tricky Turns on the Pi Network

Developed by: Adaptive Studios

Tricky Turns is a casual game deployed on the Pi Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your information when you interact with the game through the Pi Browser and related Pi services.

Information We Collect

  • Pi Network Identity: Username and public ID (via Pi Login) used for leaderboards and tipping.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, achievements, and tipping history.
  • Log Data: Browser/device type, usage timestamps, and error diagnostics.

Third-Party Services

We use official Pi Network SDKs for authentication and tipping. We do not share your data beyond what is required for these features. For more details, see the Pi Network Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This game does not directly use cookies. However, services we use may store anonymous session data. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

Use of Information

  • Authenticate users through Pi Login
  • Display scores and tipping history
  • Improve gameplay experience and fix bugs

Security

We take precautions to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Pi Network's design helps minimize personal data exposure.
